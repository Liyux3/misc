Background story:
In the year 2048, the Earth was ravaged by the outbreak of a zombie fungus, the Cordyceps Epidemic.
The infection spread rapidly, 
decimating human civilization and leaving only a handful of survivors scattered across the globe.
These survivors formed small settlements in remote areas, 
where they had access to food, water, and relative safety from the infected hordes.

Amidst the chaos, 
a small research group at the University of Hong Kong (HKU) persisted in their quest to find a cure for the Cordyceps Epidemic.
Comprised of faculty members, researchers, and students, 
this group was determined to bring hope back to humanity.

After years of tireless research, 
the HKU team finally made a major breakthrough in developing a potential cure for the fungal infection. 
However, their success did not go unnoticed.

Word spread about the research group's discovery, 
drawing the attention of a powerful gang who saw the broken world as their own twisted utopia.
They raided the HKU cure lab, 
destroying all the prototypes, equipment, 
and research data in a desperate attempt to maintain their control over the new world order.

Devastated by the loss of their work, the HKU research group's spirits were crushed.
However, a few former students from COMP2113/ENGG1340 believed that a backup copy of the cure's blueprint could still be hidden 
within the Run Run Shaw Tower on the HKU main campus.
Driven by hope and the memory of their fallen comrades, 
they decided to risk everything and venture into the zombie-infested campus to retrieve the blueprint.

After a harrowing journey through the city, only one member of the group remained -- You.
Standing before the Run Run Shaw Tower, You vowed to complete the mission, find the cure, 
and honour the sacrifices made by your friends.

Here the game begins...

Currency Description:

LabTokens

After the outbreak of the Cordyceps Epidemic, the HKU cure lab adopted an internal currency system,
known as LabTokens, to facilitate the exchange of goods and services among the remaining staff.
LabTokens were used to purchase essential items such as pharmaceuticals, armour, and weapons from automated vending machines,
ensuring that the lab members were well-equipped to face the challenges of the post-apocalyptic world.

As you explore the infested HKU campus, 
you will come across functioning vending machines that offer various items to assist them on their mission.
These machines accept LabTokens as payment, which the player can collect throughout the game by:

  # Defeating infected enemies and looting their remains
  # Solving puzzles and unlocking hidden caches of LabTokens
  # Recycling found items at designated recycling stations in exchange for LabTokens
  # With LabTokens, the main character can purchase:
  
  Pharmaceuticals: Health-restoring items such as medkits, antidotes, and stamina boosters.
Armor: Protective gear that increases the main character's resilience and provides resistance against different types of damage.
Weapons: A variety of melee and ranged weapons, each with their unique stats and abilities, allowing the player to adapt their playstyle.

You must carefully manage their LabTokens to ensure they have the right tools and resources 
to face the ever-growing threats in the infested HKU campus.
Balancing the need for better equipment and consumables will be a crucial aspect of the game, 
pushing the player to make strategic decisions throughout their journey.

Game Rules:

Use the following keys to navigate the game environment: W - Move Up A - Move Left S - Move Down D - Move Right

How to Win:

As the last hope of the research group, your mission is to navigate through the three floors of the Run Run Shaw Tower,
overcoming the hordes of infected enemies that stand in your way.
On each floor, you must defeat the zombies and solve any puzzles that arise to advance to the next level.
Successfully reach the top floor to locate and retrieve the blueprint, 
ensuring the salvation of humanity and honoring the sacrifices of your fallen comrades.
